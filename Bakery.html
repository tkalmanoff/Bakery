Sugar Mama's Pie Shop has been dedicated to homemade, handcrafted, wholesome pies since its founding in 1888. Since her first pie, Mama's bakery has used only fresh, organic ingredients to create pies the same way Mama's mama did. Sugar Mama's Pie Shop is the only place in the world where you can eat the same kind of pie, stuffed with fruits and love, that have been passed down in family recipes.
