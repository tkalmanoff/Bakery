Mama Jean learned to make pies as a small child from her mama, Mama Ruth, in Savannah, Georgia. In 1904, Mama Jean opened her own pie shop so that anyone and everyone could enjoy a slice of Mama Ruth's pie. Since the opening of our doors, Mama's Pie Shop has been dedicated to homemade, handcrafted, wholesome pies. Mama's Pie Shop still uses the same recipies, and is proud to turn out fresh pies daily that mama would've been proud of.

Continuing Mama's dedication to using local ingredients, all the fruit, eggs, and dairy used at Mama's Pie Shop are from local farms and are delivered to our door daily.

The bakers at Mama's Pie Shop are trained from the bakers that learned directly from Mama Jean herself.

Not only are our ingredients hand selected daily, but our recipies, methods, and technique come from a long and trusted line of bakers.

All our pies are hand-kneeded, rolled, and filled.

So that each of our pies is as unique as the person enjoying them!
